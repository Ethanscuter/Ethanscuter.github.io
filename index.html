Biography

I am a first-year Ph.D. Candidate/Doctoral Research Associate at the University of Hamburg, advised by Prof. Dr. Chris Biemann. Now, I'm supported by DFG Project Crossmodal Learning for Improving Human Reading. I also used to work with Prof. Wenjie Li at Hong Kong Polytechnic University and Prof. Min Yang at Chinese Academy of Sciences. Prior to that, I received the M.Eng and B.Eng. in Computer Science at South China University of Technology in 2019 and 2016 respectively.
